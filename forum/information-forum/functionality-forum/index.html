Functionality Forum

CONFERENCE HALLS IN THE INNOVATION FORUM “JOHN ATANASOFF”:

  • Main conference room (includes halls 1 + 2 + 3) – capacity up to 1000 seats (arrangement – theater type) – 837м2 – 46м x 17.90м x 6м;
  • Two conference halls (hall 1 and 3) – capacity up to 250 seats (arrangement – theater type) – 226м2 each – 12,45м x 17.90м x 6м;
  • Conference hall (hall 2) – capacity up to 450 seats (arrangement – theater type) – 385м2 – 20.90м x 17.90м x 6м ;
  • Conference hall (hall 4 at ground floor level) – capacity up to 500 seats – 516м2;
  • Open (outdoor) amphitheater – capacity up to 250 seats.

The Science and Technology Park has five conference halls in the Lab Complex with a seat capacity between 25 and 120 people.

CONFERENCE HALLS IN THE LAB COMPLEX:

  • Auditorium 1 (1st floor) with capacity up to 30 seats (arrangement: theater type) – 53м2;
  • Auditorium 2 (1st floor) with capacity up to 30 seats (arrangement: theater type) – 58м2;
  • Auditorium 3 (1st floor) with capacity up to 60 seats (arrangement: theater type) – 119м2;
  • Auditorium 4 (1st floor) with capacity up to 25 seats (arrangement: theater type) – 49м2;
  • Conference hall 125 (ground floor) with capacity up to 120 seats (arrangement: theater type) – 188м2.

In the Science and Technology Park’s conference halls  can be held eventswhich are thematically connected with the main focus areas of the Park – science, ICT, innovation, education, green energy, entrepreneurship, digitilization, health care and medicine.
Events can also be of an economic nature.
