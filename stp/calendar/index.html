Event Details

Primer is a pre-acceleration program created and certified by the European organization InnoEnergy and implemented in Bulgaria by Cleantech Bulgaria. It aims to train and prepare entrepreneurs with ideas that have business and market potential at an early stage of their development.

During the final event, 8 teams who successfully passed the program will present their innovative ideas.

You will have the opportunity to get acquainted with the funding opportunities of the start-up companies that InnoEnergy provides in 2019 as well as to learn more about green public procurement in the EU as a market opportunity for environmental and sustainable market decisions.
